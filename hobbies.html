Music

I love most types of music, but my favorites include Jazz, Classic Rock, and R&B. I have sung for most of my life in one choir or another. I was even given credit for participating on an album my church produced. I did harmony backup vocals. It is an awsome worship CD called Season of Praise. Check it out here to listen to some sound clips. Music has always been part of my life. I love rhythm's paradoxical qualities of complexity and simplicity. Rhythm is foundational, strong, and supportive. Without rhythm, there is no music, no life. As with any foundation, if it crumbles, so does everything else. I've tried out other instruments over the years, but none appeal to me as much as percussion. It is my soul instrument.
